Bio

Haley Staack, originally from the Appalachian region, haunts the hills of East Tennessee. Her magick is rooted in Appalachian folk practices and learning the art of conjure. She enjoys her simple life; connecting with the Land, remembering the old ways, and helping others connect to other realms and loved ones crossed as an Evidential/Spirit Medium. Haley loves to explore haunted places, giving the spirits a chance to tell their story. What started as one haunted clown doll lead to Haley becoming a Haunted Collector to give haunted objects a safe space to just “be”. Haley can most often be found amongst her flowers and gardens, and the spirits of the woods, as they share secrets and messages with her. Haley knows her deepest calling is to help others heal, so whether through her magick, digging and foraging from the dirt, or in her mediumship readings, she knows helping other’s heal and see the magick in themselves is pivotal.
